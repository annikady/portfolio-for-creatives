  • About Annika Yi

That journey shaped my passion for creating work with purpose and heart. Now, as a Student RGD and brand design student at Capilano University, I love building brands that make people feel good and crafting sustainable, clever packaging. If you need thoughtful, meaningful design, let's talk.
